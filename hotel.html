WELCOME TO GAUTAMI HEIGHTS


Gautami Heights is located at a distance of 58.5 km from the Pantnagar Airport and 21 km from the Pirumadara Railway Station. The popular tourist attractions like Drona Sagar (2.7 km), Chaiti Devi Temple (1.6 km), Giri Sarovar (4.2 km) and many others are situated withi close proximity to the hotel. Apart from its prime location and magnificent surroundings, the hotel offers a wide range of guest facilities like internet, room service, front desk and parking space. Hotel houses spacious and beautifully decorated rooms, which ensure guests a comfortable stay and are equipped with contemporary conveniences such as television, air conditioning, telephone, mini bar and attached bathrooms with hot/cold water and essential toiletries. Relish authentic and flavourful dishes in the contemporary yet ethnic ambience served at the in-house restaurant. Ease out and have a lovely stay at Gautami Heights!

Hotel Rooms





  Hotel room    facilities



  1. Comfortable Bed: A hotel room typically includes a comfortable bed with clean linens, pillows, and blankets.
  2. Private Bathroom: Most hotel rooms have a private bathroom equipped with a shower or bathtub, toilet, sink, and amenities such as towels, soap, shampoo, and conditioner.
  3. Furniture: You can expect to find furniture in the room, including a desk or table, chairs, and a wardrobe or closet for storing your clothes and belongings.
  4. Television: A television is often provided in hotel rooms, offering a range of channels for entertainment.
  5. Air Conditioning/Heating: Hotels usually provide temperature control options to ensure your comfort, such as air conditioning or heating units.
  6. Wi-Fi: Most hotels offer complimentary Wi-Fi access in their rooms, allowing you to stay connected and browse the internet.
  7. Telephone: A telephone may be available in the room, enabling you to make local and international calls, as well as contact hotel staff.
  8. Minibar/Fridge: Some hotel rooms have a minibar or a small fridge stocked with beverages and snacks. However, please note that these items may come at an additional cost.
  9. Safe: Many hotels provide an in-room safe where you can securely store your valuables such as passports, cash, and electronics.
  10. Coffee/Tea Making Facilities: Some hotels provide a kettle or coffee maker with complimentary coffee and tea sachets for your convenience.
  11. Iron and Ironing Board: In case you need to freshen up your clothes, hotels often offer an iron and ironing board in the room or upon request.
  12. Hairdryer: A hairdryer is commonly provided in the bathroom for your use.

Here are some common facilities you might find in a dining area:


1. Tables and Chairs:

The dining area typically consists of a variety of tables and chairs to accommodate different group sizes, from small tables for couples to larger tables for families or groups.

2. Buffet or Serving Stations:

In some dining areas, there may be buffet or serving stations where guests can choose from a variety of dishes and serve themselves. These stations often include a selection of appetizers, main courses, side dishes, and desserts.

3. Bar Area:

Some dining areas include a bar or a separate counter where guests can order drinks, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. It may feature a wide selection of beverages, including wines, spirits, cocktails, and specialty drinks.

4. Kitchen or Food Preparation Area:

Behind the scenes, a well-equipped kitchen or food preparation area is essential for the dining area to function smoothly. This is where chefs and kitchen staff prepare and cook the meals, ensuring they meet the highest standards of taste and presentation.

5. Restrooms:

To provide convenience and comfort to guests, dining areas often have restrooms available nearby. These facilities are well-maintained, clean, and easily accessible.

6. Lighting and Ambiance:

The dining area may feature a variety of lighting options to create a desired atmosphere. From soft, dimmed lighting for a romantic setting to brighter lighting for a more energetic ambiance, careful consideration is given to lighting fixtures and their placement.

7. Sound System:

Many dining areas have a sound system in place to provide background music or entertainment, enhancing the overall dining experience. The volume and type of music played can be adjusted to match the desired atmosphere.

8. Decor and Aesthetics:

Aesthetics play a vital role in creating an inviting and visually pleasing dining area. This may include carefully chosen furniture, decorative elements, artwork, plants, and other design features that contribute to the overall ambiance.

9. Staff and Service:

A well-trained and attentive staff is essential for a dining area to provide excellent service. This includes servers, hosts/hostesses, bartenders, and other support staff who ensure that guests are attended to promptly and professionally.

10. Accessibility:

Consideration is given to accessibility features, such as wheelchair ramps, elevators, or designated seating areas, to accommodate guests with disabilities and ensure an inclusive dining experience.

Remember , the facilities in a dining area may vary depending on the type of establishment, its size, and the specific dining experience it aims to provide.
